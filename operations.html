General Operations

  • Enable Tournament Mode: Disables undo and viewing of removed tiles. Feature is incomplete
  • New Game: Starts a new game with a new tileset.
  • Restart: Restarts the current board.
  • Numbered Game: Play a seeded game of MahJong.
  • Undo Stack: Displays tiles removed from the board.
  • Toggle Sound: Turns off sound. Sound is initially set to on.
